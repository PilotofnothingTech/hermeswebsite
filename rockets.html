Enarxis — Czech Rocket Challenge 2025

Water‑payload class rocket designed for reliability, low cost, and repeatable performance in our debut year.

Length830 mm
Body Diameter49 mm
Dry Mass675 g
Lift‑off Mass1062 g
Predicted Apogee~691 m
Descent Velocity~6.3 m/s
Primary MaterialABS+ (3D printed)
Systems overview
  • Airframe: 3D‑printed ABS+ structure with bayonet twist‑lock bulkheads; fins bonded with cyanoacrylate; dual rail buttons.
  • Payload (water): ~216 ml (~216 g) flexible bladder with spacer and compression bulkhead to minimize sloshing.
  • Recovery: spring‑loaded ejection with servo‑latched pin; toroidal main parachute; backup deploy if no apogee within 15 s.
  • Avionics: Arduino Nano 33 BLE Sense Rev2, SD logger, SX1278 LoRa 433 MHz, 2S Li‑ion, 3.3 V regulator, buzzer + driver.
  • Ground Station: matching SX1278 link via Raspberry Pi Pico W bridge; high‑gain loaded antenna.
