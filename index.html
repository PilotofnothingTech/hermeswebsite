Rocket — Enarxis

CRC 2025 water‑payload rocket designed for reliability and simplicity: low cost, easy to manufacture, and repeatable performance. Built by students, flown by procedure.

Length830 mm
Body Diameter49 mm
Dry Mass675 g
Lift‑off Mass1062 g
Expected Apogee691 m
Descent Speed~6.3 m/s
Systems overview
  • Airframe: 3D‑printed ABS+ structure, bayonet twist‑lock bulkheads, rail buttons; fins bonded with cyanoacrylate.
  • Payload (water): 216 ml (~216 g) enclosed in flexible bladder with spacer to minimize slosh; compartment compression to lock payload.
  • Avionics: Arduino Nano 33 BLE Sense Rev2, SD module, SX1278 LoRa 433 MHz, 2S Li‑ion battery, 3.3 V regulator, buzzer & driver.
  • Recovery: spring‑loaded ejection with servo‑latched pin; toroidal parachute; backup deploy if no apogee after 15 s.
  • Ground station: matching SX1278 LoRa link via Raspberry Pi Pico W bridge; high‑gain loaded antenna.
