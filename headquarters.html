Project Hermes Headquarters

Located at the University of the Peloponnese in Patras, our HQ combines design labs, fabrication, and test spaces so students can take ideas from CAD to flight‑ready hardware.

Spaces & capabilities

Design Lab

High‑spec workstations, CAD/CFD toolchain, versioned data and reviews.

Manufacturing Bay

Additive (Bambu X1C, Creality K1C), light machining, composites bench, QA.

Avionics Bench

Soldering, fixtures, telemetry link tests, SD logging, sensor checkout.

Recovery & Parachutes

Spring‑ejection rigs, canopy sewing table, drop‑test prep.

Mission Control

Ground‑station laptops, radios, procedures, and rehearsals.

Collab Space

Team stand‑ups, design reviews, and sponsor meetings.
